Browser Storage Abuser


Have you ever wondered how much storage you can use on browsers? This tool is for you to abuse and experiment with limit of your favorite storage!

  • Drag and drop files and folders onto your favorite storage.
  • "Upload" files by selection.
  • "Fill Storage" with blobs with arbitrary size.
